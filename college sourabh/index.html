College of Engineering Karunagappally

The College of Engineering Karunagappally (CEK) is a public institute of engineering and technology located in Karunagappally, Kollam district, Kerala, India. Established in 1999 by the Government of Kerala, it is the second engineering college in Kollam district and the fourth under the Institute of Human Resources Development in Electronics.

The college has a strong focus on technical education and has made its presence felt in the state's technical horizon. For more information on admissions, courses, and rankings, you can visit the college's website or contact them directly.
