About Kerala

Kerala is a beautiful state located in the southwestern region of India. Known for its palm-lined beaches, backwaters, hill stations, and vibrant culture, it offers a unique blend of nature and tradition.

Culture

The culture of Kerala is deeply rooted in dance, music, festivals, and classical arts. Traditional dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, along with festivals such as Onam and Vishu, reflect its rich heritage.

Food

Kerala's cuisine is rich and flavorful, often featuring coconut, rice, and spices. Famous dishes include Kerala Sadya, Porotta and Beef, Appam with stew, Puttu, and spicy seafood curries.
